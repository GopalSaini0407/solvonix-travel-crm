Common Travel Documents Staff Usually Collect

Domestic Trips

Aadhaar or government ID, PAN for finance, flight tickets, hotel vouchers, payment receipt and emergency contact sheet.

International Trips

Passport, visa copy, PAN, passport-size photo, travel insurance, flight ticket, hotel voucher and compliance papers.

Family / Minor Travelers

Birth certificate or school ID for minors, consent letter when needed, and each traveler's ID mapped clearly.
